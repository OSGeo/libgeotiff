EPSG Notes

Since 1972 a modified form of the two standard parallel case has been used in Belgium. For the Lambert Conic Conformal (2 SP Belgium), the formulas for the standard two standard parallel case given above are used except for: 

	Easting, E = EF + r sin (* - a)
	Northing, N = NF + rF - r cos (* - a)
and for the reverse formulas
	* = ((*' + a)/n) +*0
where a = 29.2985 seconds.","For Projected Coordinate System Belge l972 / Belge Lambert 72
